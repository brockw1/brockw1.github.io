Dr. Sphinx

Dr. Sphinx is the evil villain in the HABIT VR project I am currently working on for my Senior Design project. In this project, he escapes from his prison cell and sends the player on a chase, forcing them to solve the many puzzles Dr. Sphinx makes if the player wants to catch him.

I modeled Dr. Sphinx in Cinema 4D and then exported the model to be used in Unity. Our HABIT VR team wanted a villain that was simple in design to make animation easier to implement as many team members have little experience in animation. I took inspiration from other villains across video games such as Dr. Neo Cortex from the Crash Bandicoot series and Waluigi from the Super Mario Bros. series.

In addition to the Dr. Sphinx model, I also modeled some custom assets for a variety of puzzles in the HABIT VR project. I will include some of these below as they are made.
