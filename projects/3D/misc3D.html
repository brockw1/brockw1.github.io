Here is a collection of other 3D projects & animations I have made. All projects on this page were modeled or animated in Cinema 4D.

Myconid

March 2019. My first character sculpting project. This bust is a mushroom person, based off of Myconids from Dungeons and Dragons as well as Groot from Guardians of the Galaxy. I learned a lot about mapping and sculpting (and the struggles that come along with that).

this is mud.

February 2019. For my Computer Graphics course we were challenged to model a creature based off of a child's drawing. This was the result of that.

Retro Mouse

January 2019. One of my very first 3D modeling projects for my Computer Graphics course. We were given an old computer mouse, and tasked with recreating it.

Sword and Stone

January 2019. Another very early 3D modeling project for my Computer Graphics course.

Apple Charger

January 2019. The very first 3D modeling project we had for my Computer Graphics course. We were shown easy ways to make something simple look very realistic, using HDRI lighting.
