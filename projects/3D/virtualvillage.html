Virtual Village

Virtual Village was my final project for ARTS 291Q - Introduction to 3D Computer Graphics. In this final project, we were tasked with creating and animating a floating island that had at least one character and structure on the island.

Virtual Village takes inspiration from the many artifacts of early 2000s web pages. I looked to imagine what world might exist in the vast variety of GIFs that plagued early blogs of the 2000s. The characters and world I ended up with also takes inspiration from games like Animal Crossing. I wanted the world to look toy-like and so I made sure materials reflected plastic and vinyl toy sets. I modeled, textured, and animated in Cinema4D and edited the final video in Adobe After Effects.
